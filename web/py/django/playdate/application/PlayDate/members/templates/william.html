Hi! My name is William Plachno. My pronouns are he and him.

I am a 32 year old computer science major who lives in the Santa Cruz mountains. I am married to my wife, Jessica Stangle, and have two cats, Ada and Eris.

After graduating high school in 2008, I started taking classes at Sonoma State University, where I made a lot of friends, but quickly let may academic side drop in priority. Between some not-so-great grades, as well as the rising cost of tuition, my parents allowed me to move in with them. I spent the next section of my life re-examining who I was and who I wanted to be, and started going to community college while doing an internship at Kaseya IT Management Solutions. It turns out that what grades I had earned at Sonoma wouldn't fully transfer, so I had to start from scratch.

I passed Calculus, a major milestone for my educational career, and found myself truly enjoying school. Before too long, I earned an Associates degree in Businesss and CIS, then a transfer degree, which allowed my return to a four year college.

I am now taking CSC648, Sofware Engineering, in a summer session at San Francisco State University, and only have one more semester afterwards before I earn a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

While taking classes, I also am the Games Manager at Illusive Comics and Games, where I coordinate logistics of board games and card games. As a Computer Science major, I have a respect and love for systems of all kinds. This is why I love modern board games design, where the player needs to strategize and coordinate multiple touch points of a set of interconnected systems to claim victory.
If you have any questions about my cats, my life, or what a 'Roland Wright' is, feel free to e-mail me at wjplachno@gmail.com .

Thank you for reading!
